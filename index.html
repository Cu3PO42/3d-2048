User Manual


The goal of this game is to join all the numbers and reach 2048.

You move all the squares at once. When two squares with the same number collide,

they merge into one, producing a square with their values added!

The game is over when you’re unable to proceed.


You begin with two squares. When making a move,

a new square with a value of either two or four spawns randomly.

You have two options of moving the squares:


1. Left-click:

Pushes the squares away from the cursor (if possible)

2. Right-Click:

Pulls the squares towards the cursor (if possible)

You are able to control your position by using the arrow-keys and the pace with the scrollbar in the lower right corner.

PLAY

  • Tips:

    1. Don’t rush

    In this game time doesn’t have any impact on your score.

    So take your time to think through your moves.

    Keep in mind that every move spawns another number.

    2. Create Rows

    You want the big numbers as close as possible.Keep in mind:

    most of the times you still have a chance to bring the numbers together,

    even when they are all over the place.

    3. Anti-Claustrophobic

    Empty squares are the be all and end all of the game.

    They can either help you achieve your personal goal….or make your life hell.

    Whilst it makes sense to collect large numbers in one row,

    an empty square in between them could mess with your progress,

    if a new number happens to spawn there.
